There's something going on every night!


Water Ski Show- Sunday Night
With roots as deep as the Aquarama Ski show at the CNE and as far as Australia, the Fern Resort Ski show is great fun for the whole family. Demonstrating classic skiing styles as well as radical wakeboarding, the show’s action and fun will be loved by everyone.
Dine-In Movie- Sunday Night
Pop corn, soft drinks, pool and fantastic flicks; what more could you ask for. Watch the movie of the evening while sitting comfortably in a noodle in the large outdoor pool or for those that want a less "wet" environment you can join us in Bergwen's. A different way to enjoy the movies, with a summer twist, that is sure to be fun for all ages.
Jamie Williams- Monday Night
Jamie Williams has been performing professionally for over 25 years. As a headline performer for Fern for many years, he has entertained audiences of people from all across North America. After his shows the response is always the same - "Wow! How does one guy produce such a big sound?"
Reality Fern- Tuesday Night
Get ready to experience the best of reality TV. Reality Fern is an evening that will immerse you in the best of reality TV. Test your skills at Duck Dynasty, Donut Showdown or Swamp People. Cash cab will be swinging by to test your trivia knowledge and all sorts of “So You Think You Can???”
Bob Cates Comedy in Motion- Wednesday Night
Bob Cates has been characterized with this title and many more. He is a professional comic variety entertainer with an amazingly funny and clean show which is perfect for the whole family. With over 13 years of performing experience, many awards, and thousands of satisfied customers, he delivers! He’s a comic with big talent, and Canadian charm. Bob was fittingly awarded Best Entertainer of the Year at the 1999 and 2001 Molson Canadian FEO AWARDS..
Staff Lip Sync Show- Thursday Night
The talented participants in our guests’ most revered entertainment, the Fern Resort Staff Lip Sync Show, are from many departments on the resort. The performance mixes review style dance routines and impersonations with great comic energy. The result is a spirited and hilarious 75-90 minute show that you are sure to love!
Magic of Richard Forget- Friday Night
Richard Forget is an international award-winning magician that has created a magnificent magical experience that is inspired by his years traveling throughout the world and living in Toronto. Richard’s vignettes tell a fascinating tale about Richard’s experiences in an urban setting and in a modern world, with a magical twist.
Hypnotist Richard Cole- Saturday Night
Ontario comedy hypnotist Richard Cole provided the ultimate in audience participation. Don’t measure this performance by any other hypnosis show you have seen. Richard has a team that helps him create a new show every year. From the very first skit to the bows, you can feel confident that you’ll see things you never thought you would see.
